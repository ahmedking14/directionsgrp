Core Deliverables

Our deliverables are designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing tools like Core365 and Go High Level, providing immediate value through AI-driven automation.

1. Lead Follow-Up & Retargeting Automation

Automate the nurturing of non-closed leads to maximize conversions and minimize manual effort.

  • Set up automated drip campaigns across SMS, email, and push notifications for personalized follow-ups.
  • Auto-classify lead outcomes (e.g., won, lost, no-show) and trigger tailored workflows.
  • Re-engagement sequences including messaging and scheduling links to recapture interest.
  • Full integration with Core365 & Go High Level for real-time lead syncing and centralized management.

Benefits:

  • Increases close rates from warm leads by 30-50%.
  • Saves 20-30 hours/week in manual follow-up tasks.
  • Prevents lost revenue from forgotten or overlooked leads, especially in high-volume sectors like solar.
Lead Recovery Rate: +35%

2. Onboarding & Offboarding Automation (Field Reps)

Streamline hiring and transitions for field representatives with AI-powered funnels, ensuring rapid scaling and compliance.

  • Build an onboarding funnel with automated emails, form-fills, training resource delivery, and credential provisioning.
  • Offboarding trigger system for role removal, device logouts, and status updates in Core365.
  • Re-engagement automation for "ghosted" applicants via drip campaigns and reminders.

Benefits:

  • Handles high-volume hiring efficiently, reducing administrative overhead.
  • Cuts onboarding time by 60%, getting reps productive faster.
  • Improves retention by 20% through a professional, smooth first impression and ramp-up process.
Onboarding Efficiency: 60% Faster

3. Cross-Service Lead Looping

Intelligent AI-driven system to loop leads between services (e.g., roofing to solar or pest control), maximizing cross-selling opportunities and revenue from existing leads.

  • AI analysis of lead profiles to identify cross-service fit based on data like location, interests, and past interactions.
  • Automated triggers to route non-converting leads to alternative services with personalized pitches.
  • Seamless integration with Core365 & Go High Level for tracking looped leads and performance metrics.
  • Customizable looping rules and A/B testing for optimization across verticals.

Benefits:

  • Unlocks additional revenue streams by converting 15-25% of "lost" leads into wins in other services.
  • Enhances customer lifetime value through targeted cross-selling without manual intervention.
  • Reduces lead acquisition costs by repurposing existing traffic across your portfolio.
Cross-Sell Conversion: +20%
